# 🦷 DentalLab SaaS: Comprehensive User Manual

This manual provides a detailed, step-by-step guide for all users of the DentalLab SaaS platform.

---

## 1. Introduction
DentalLab SaaS is a professional management platform designed to streamline the workflow between Dental Laboratories and Doctors. It features real-time case tracking, inventory management, and automated invoicing.

---

## 2. User Roles & Access Levels
*   **Super Admin:** Platform-wide control, manages all labs, and views global statistics.
*   **Lab Admin:** Manages a specific lab, handles billing, inventory, and staff/client approvals.
*   **Doctor (Client):** Submits cases, tracks progress, and manages their own invoices.

---

## 3. Step-by-Step: Getting Started

### 3.1 Login
1.  Navigate to the application URL.
2.  Click **"Sign in with Google"**.
3.  Upon first login, your profile will be created automatically.

### 3.2 Lab Branding (Admins Only)
1.  Navigate to **Settings** in the sidebar.
2.  Select the **Branding** tab.
3.  **Upload Logo:** Click "Select File" to upload your lab's logo (PNG/SVG, <500KB).
4.  **Set Colors:** Use the color pickers to choose your **Primary** and **Accent** colors.
5.  Click **Save Changes**. The platform will immediately update to reflect your brand.

### 3.3 Building Your Network
1.  Navigate to **Doctors** (to invite clients).
2.  Click the **"Invite"** button.
3.  Enter the user's email address and assign their role.
4.  Click **"Send Invitation"**.

### 3.4 Approving New Users
1.  When a user accepts an invite and registers, they appear in the **Approvals** tab.
2.  Review the request and click **"Approve"**.
3.  The user will now have full access to their dashboard.

---

## 4. Step-by-Step: Case Management

### 4.1 Creating a New Case (Doctors)
1.  Go to the **Cases** tab.
2.  Click **"Add New Case"**.
3.  Fill in the **Patient Name**, **Tooth Number**, and **Case Type** (e.g., Crown).
4.  Select a **Due Date** and add any specific **Notes**.
5.  Click **"Create Case"**.

### 4.2 Production Workflow (Lab Admins)
1.  View assigned cases on your **Dashboard**.
2.  **Start Work:** Change the case status from `Received` to `In Progress`.
3.  **Technical Details:** Click on a case to view specific instructions or notes.
4.  **Completion:** Once the prosthetic is finished, update the status to `Ready`.

---

## 5. Step-by-Step: Inventory & Materials

### 5.1 Adding Stock (Admins)
1.  Navigate to the **Inventory** tab.
2.  Click **"Add Item"**.
3.  Enter the material name (e.g., Zirconia Block A2), category, and unit (pcs, grams).
4.  Set a **Min Threshold** (e.g., 5). The system will alert you when stock falls below this number.
5.  Enter the **Cost per Unit** and **Supplier** details.

### 5.2 Recording Material Usage
1.  In the **Inventory** tab, click **"Record Usage"**.
2.  **Select Item:** Choose the material used.
3.  **Associate Case:** Select the active case the material was used for.
4.  **Quantity:** Enter the amount used.
5.  Click **"Record Usage"**. The stock level will decrease automatically.

---

## 6. Step-by-Step: Invoicing & Payments

### 6.1 Generating an Invoice (Admins)
1.  Navigate to the **Invoices** tab.
2.  Click **"Generate Invoice"**.
3.  Select a **Doctor**. The system will automatically list all cases marked as `Ready` that haven't been billed yet.
4.  Select the cases you wish to include.
5.  Click **"Create Invoice"**.

### 6.2 Managing Payments
1.  View all invoices in the **Invoices** list.
2.  Once payment is received, click the **Status** badge to toggle it from `Pending` to `Paid`.

---

## 7. Reports & Analytics
1.  Navigate to **Reports**.
2.  **Revenue:** View your monthly earnings and projected income.
3.  **Volume:** Track how many cases are being processed per week/month.
4.  **Performance:** Identify your most loyal clinic clients.

---

## 8. Troubleshooting
*   **Permission Denied:** Ensure your Lab Admin has approved your account in the "Approvals" tab.
*   **Low Stock Alerts:** Check the Dashboard for red warning icons indicating materials that need reordering.
*   **Missing Logo:** If your logo doesn't appear, ensure you clicked "Save Changes" in the Branding settings.
